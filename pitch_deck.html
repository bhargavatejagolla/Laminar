Abstract & Problem Statement

The Challenge

Modern cities generate vast amounts of data (surveillance, traffic, IoT), but systems operate in isolated silos.

Fragmentation leads to delayed emergency responses, disjointed decision-making, and inefficient incident management.

Existing setups are purely reactive, relying on post-incident analysis rather than proactive intelligence.

The Objective

To architect a unified intelligence platform that can observe, predict, and autonomously coordinate city-wide responses in real-time, functioning as the city's central nervous system.

Target Users & Impact

  • City Administration & Control Centers
  • Police & Law Enforcement
  • Emergency & Medical Responders

Proposed Solution

Laminar is an ultra-responsive, full-stack AI OS fusing Computer Vision, LLMs, and 4D spatial analytics.

Real-Time Vision

YOLOv11-powered object detection, cross-camera crowd density tracking, and anomaly recognition across thousands of live streams.

Randy AI & Gemini

Embedded AI Assistant capable of contextual reasoning, executing natural language video search, and generating tactical incident dossiers instantly.

Immersive 3D UI

Next-gen React Three Fiber dashboard offering spatial intelligence mapping and interactive data visualization for command operators.

Breakthrough Innovation

Zero-Shot Behavioral Intelligence: Predicts panic dispersal and aggressive altercations before they escalate without prior explicit training.

AI Green Wave: Autonomous traffic signal synchronization prioritizing ambulances and firetrucks dynamically to clear paths instantly.

Methodology & Workflow

1. Data Ingestion & Edge Sensing

Aggregates live multi-venue camera streams, IoT sensor data, and infrastructure telemetry. Utilizes WebSockets for ultra-low latency transmission.

2. Edge Processing & Vision AI

YOLOv11 and OpenCV process frames at the edge. Performs structural health monitoring, flood segmentation, and crowd velocity tracking simultaneously.

3. Predictive AI & LLM Fusion

Data flows into Google Gemini API and Prophet models to map escalation probabilities, execute temporal intelligence, and generate automated threat dossiers.

4. Autonomous Action & Command

System triggers AEGIS protocols: automated SMTP escalation to police, Live Safe-Pathing for civilians, and dynamic alerts on the 3D dashboard.
